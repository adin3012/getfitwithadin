The Process

How It Works

Simple, structured, and entirely over WhatsApp. No app to download. No new habit to build just to access the coaching.

1

Data Collection

The first 7 days are dedicated to gathering your details: weight, height, age, activity level, goals, and training history. No guesswork.

2

Personalised Plans

Based on your data, I build a customised training plan, nutrition plan, and supplement guidance specific to your goals and schedule.

3

Open Communication

You have direct access to me via calls or WhatsApp at any point. Questions, doubts, off days. I am available throughout the programme.

4

Frequent Check-Ins

Regular progress tracking with footage reviews and measurement updates. I adjust your plans based on real results, not assumptions.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to go to a gym?

No. I design programmes for home, gym, or wherever you prefer. If you have 30 minutes and a floor, we can work with that.

How does coaching actually happen?

Everything is over WhatsApp. You receive a weekly check-in link every Monday and have direct access to me. Simple as that.

Will this work with Indian food?

Absolutely. I eat Indian food. My clients eat Indian food. Sustainable nutrition is about principles, not cuisine restrictions.

How much time do I need daily?

The check-in takes three minutes a week. Workouts can be 30 to 45 minutes. This is designed around your schedule, not an imaginary ideal.

Do you only coach in Guwahati?

No. I coach clients across India entirely via WhatsApp. Location does not matter at all.

What is the final price?

Prices vary based on your goals and current situation. The ranges shown are a guide. Reach out and I will give you a clear number after a short conversation.

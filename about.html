About Me

Adin Ankur Saikia

Fitness Coach, Corporate Professional, Natural Bodybuilder, Marathon Runner

I was 117kg during engineering. Not slightly overweight. 117kg. I tried every diet and workout plan available and nothing worked until I stopped chasing shortcuts and started learning the actual science of fat loss, nutrition, and training.

That journey led me across multiple arenas. I competed at ICN Hyderabad 2025, a national drug-tested natural bodybuilding show, and won gold. I finished Devil's Circuit, one of India's most demanding obstacle course races. I completed multiple 10km marathons. All while holding a full-time corporate job in IT governance and compliance.

None of it was done with unlimited time or a professional athlete's schedule. It was done with the same 24 hours you have. That is the entire point.

Since then I have helped 50 plus people transform. Corporate employees, founders, and agency owners who told me they had no time. That excuse did not work for me and it will not work for them either.

The Journey

117kg to Gold

2019

Started Engineering, The Heavy Years

Joined KIIT University, Bhubaneswar for B.Tech in IT. Sedentary college life, poor food habits, zero fitness awareness. Weight steadily climbed toward 117kg.

2021

117kg, The Turning Point

Hit peak weight of 117kg. Tried multiple diets and workout plans and all failed because none were built on real science. Started researching nutrition and training principles seriously.

2022

The Transformation Begins

Built a proper system with structured training, evidence-based nutrition, and consistent weekly tracking. Started seeing real results for the first time. Began informally helping friends transform.

2023

Graduated, Entered Corporate Life

Completed B.Tech and joined the corporate world. Continued training and began coaching people independently, proving the system worked beyond just my own results.

2024

Scaling the Coaching Practice

Growing roster of clients. Built CoachFlow, my own coaching CRM, to manage weekly check-ins, body measurements, and progress tracking. Began competition preparation.

2025

Gold Medal, Devil's Circuit, Marathons and 50 Plus Clients

Won gold at ICN Hyderabad 2025, a national drug-tested natural bodybuilding championship. Finished Devil's Circuit. Completed multiple 10km marathons. 50 plus clients transformed. The system, fully proven.
